Lays of Belariand


A lay is a short romance, written in verse. These lays cover some of the epic tales of the land of Beleriand, a country in Tolkien's Middle-Earth. This land was lost at the end of the first age, but the impact of the events lived on into the Lord of the Rings. It is the third book of the history of Middle-Earth.
